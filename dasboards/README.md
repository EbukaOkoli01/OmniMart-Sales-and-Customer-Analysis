This folder contains the interactive Excel dashboards created to visualize insights from the OmniMart dataset.
Two main dashboards were developed to analyze sales performance and customer behavior between 2005â€“2008.

## 1. Sales Dashboard

- Displays Total Revenue, Profit, Cost, and Quantity

- Highlights Top 5 Selling Products and Top 3 Countries by Revenue

- Shows Year-over-Year (YoY) and Month-over-Month (MoM) Revenue Trends

- Includes slicers for dynamic filtering by Country and Quarter

## 2. Customer Dashboard

- Focuses on customer segmentation and churn analysis

- Includes KPIs such as Total Customers, Average Revenue per Customer, and Gender Split

- Visualizes churn distribution (Active, At-Risk, About-to-Churn)

- Revenue breakdown by Age Group, Education, Income Level, and Gender

- Includes slicers for dynamic filtering by Salary Category, Education, Age Group, Gender, and Number of Children

- Each dashboard is designed to help OmniMart understand sales trends, identify at-risk customers, and improve retention strategies.
